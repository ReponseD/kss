Kagarama Secondary School: A Place of Discovery and Growth

At Kagarama Secondary School, we believe that education is a journey of discovery and growth. Our vibrant community is dedicated to empowering students to realize their full potential through a blend of rigorous academics, a culture of respect, and a commitment to inclusivity.

Our Mission

We strive to inspire and equip our students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and compassionate leaders who contribute meaningfully to their communities and the world at large.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school in the region, recognized for our commitment to academic rigor, innovative teaching methods, and a nurturing environment that promotes student well-being, creativity, and leadership development.

Core Values
