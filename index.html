About Us

Kagarama Secondary School is more than just a school; it's a vibrant community dedicated to fostering academic excellence, nurturing individual talents, and preparing students for a successful future. We believe in providing a holistic education that empowers students to become confident, responsible, and compassionate global citizens.

Academics

At Kagarama, we offer a challenging and diverse curriculum designed to inspire intellectual curiosity and prepare students for the demands of the 21st century. From STEM subjects to the arts, we provide students with a wide range of opportunities to explore their interests and develop essential skills for success.

Admissions

Are you ready to embark on an educational journey that will transform your life? At Kagarama Secondary School, we believe in fostering a supportive and dynamic learning environment where students can thrive. Apply today and join our community of dedicated learners and passionate educators.

Student Life

At Kagarama, student life is a vibrant tapestry of extracurricular activities, social events, and opportunities for leadership development. From sports teams and clubs to student government and community service initiatives, there's something for everyone.
